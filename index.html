About Me

Dr. Yasar Abbas Ur Rehman received his Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from the City University of Hong Kong, in 2019. During his Ph.D. study, he worked under the supervision Dr. Lai-Man Po on Face Liveness detection using Deep Learning techniques. Prior to his Ph.D. degree, he worked in NuSyS Lab under the supervision of Dr. Muhammad Tariq on Wireless Multimedia Sensor Networks (WMSN). He is currently working with TCL Corporate Research (Hong Kong) co., Limited as an Algorithm Specialist.

Dr. Yasar was the receipient of the full-time Ph.D Studentship (2016-2019), Research Tuition Scholarship (2018-2019), and Best Academic Performance Award (2018-2019), respectively of the City University of Hong Kong. He received Bronze Medal in M.Sc degree in electrical engineering from the FAST National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, Pakistan (2013-2015), and Gold Medal in B.Sc electrical engineering (Telecommunication) from the City University of Science and Information Technology, Pakistan (2008-2012).
